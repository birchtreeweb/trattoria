About us

Bruno opened his first restaurant 25 years ago and since then he is happy to serve the best pizza in town in his 3 resraurants.

Trattoria Italiana is family restaurant which has started with 5 tables and lots of love. Giorgio always loved the food his grandma cooked for him when he was little and he started cooking the Italian cuisine long time ago.

Bruno now has an amazing team and he cooks himself in his restaurants for the hungry people in town.

Trattoria Italiana is a favourite place for people who are in love with Italy and food. You can taste delicious MIlanese pepperoni pizza, tagliatelle from Napoli and freshly baked ciabatta.
