About us

Bruno opened his first restaurant 25 years ago and since then he is happy to serve the best Italian, seafood, and steak in town in his 2 restaurants.

Trattoria Italiana by Bruno is a family restaurant which has started with 20 tables and lots of love. Bruno has always had a passion for locally sourced vegetables, seafood, chicken and steak, and he started cooking fine Italian cuisine a long time ago.

Now Bruno wants to share this passion for great Italian food at an affordable price. He has an amazing team and cooks himself in his restaurants for people in search of a unique dining experience.

Trattoria Italiana by Bruno is a favorite place for people who are in love with Italy and food. The menu is less formal than a ristorante, but truly authentic.
